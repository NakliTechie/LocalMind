About LocalMind

A private AI chatbot running Gemma entirely in your browser via WebGPU.

  • No server — all inference runs locally
  • No API keys — the model runs in your tab
  • No data leaves your device — ever

Models:

  • Gemma 3 1B (~760 MB) — fast, text-only
  • Gemma 4 E2B (~1.5 GB) — multimodal (image + audio)
  • Gemma 4 E4B (~4.9 GB) — multimodal, best quality

Multimodal: With Gemma 4 models, attach images (upload, paste, camera) or audio (mic, file). MP4 video is experimental — frames and audio are extracted separately.

Models are cached after first download — future visits load instantly.

Requirements: Chrome 113+, Edge 113+, or Firefox 130+ with WebGPU.

Powered by Transformers.js + Google Gemma (Apache 2.0).
